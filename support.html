Support Signal

Or donate with cryptocurrency

The Signal team is committed to our mission of developing open-source privacy technologies that protect freedom of expression and create a safe global communication environment. Your donation promotes this. No ads. No tracking. No kidding.

Your donation helps us pay for the development, servers, and bandwidth costs of an application used by millions of people around the world to communicate privately and instantly.

When you provide your email address, you'll receive a confirmation email for your tax file. The Signal Technology Foundation is an independent nonprofit organization and is tax-exempt under section 501c3 of the Internal Revenue Code. Our Federal Tax ID number is 82-4506840.

Note: Signal can't send you badges if you donate this way instead of signing in with Google Pay or Apple Pay in the app.

Donate with Cryptocurrency

Donations to Signal in cryptocurrency will be sent through The Giving Block"

If you'd like to receive a U.S. tax deduction on the value of your cryptocurrency donation, you can choose to provide an email address to receive a tax receipt. The Giving Block also supports you to make anonymous donations.
