Share Not Afraid of Vulnerabilities

Advanced end-to-end encryption (powered by the open-source Signal Protocol) to keep your conversations safe. We can't read your messages or hear your calls, and no one else can. Privacy isn't an optional mode — it's simply how Signal works. Every message, every call, every time.

Say anything

Share text, voice messages, images, videos, animations and files for free. Signal uses your mobile data connection so you can avoid SMS and MMS charges.

Speak freely

Make ultra-clear voice and video calls with anyone who lives across the city, or across the ocean, without the cost of long-distance calling.

Privacy & Stickets

Add an expressive layer to your conversations with coded stickers. You can also create and share your own sticker sets.

Meet through Groups

Group chat helps you stay in touch with family, friends, and colleagues easily.

No ads. No tracking. No kidding.

Signal doesn't have any third-party advertisers, and doesn't track users. So keep focusing on the moments that matter to you to the people who matter.

Free for everyone

Signal is an independent non-profit organization. We have no affiliation with big tech companies, and we will never be bought by these companies. Signal development is supported by grants and donations from individuals like you.
